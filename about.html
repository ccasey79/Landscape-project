About

Locally Owned and Family Operated


A&H Landscapes is a family run business which started out in 2002. Nathan and Paul Streetly created the company as a father / son business, but as the company has progressed it now employs over 12 employee's. Pretty much every type of landscaping job you can think of has been worked on over the companies duration. We're really proud with how far the company has come since we started. The company is based in Northampton but we cover all the Midlands area. If it's a large landscaping project we are open to it nationally, feel free to enquire! We've been fortuate to have some really talented staff over the years, a special thanks to their hard work and dedication on behalf of the company.


We offer our clients...

  2. 2 - Will always strive to add that bit extra to any job we do

  3. 3 - We cover all areas of landscaping

  4. 4 - We specialise in water features and decking projects
